Keep Your Screen On


This page will keep your PC screen on as long as it is open.

Note: Keeping the screen on for extended periods can drain your battery quickly.



Quick Guide for "Keep Your Screen On" Webpage


Open the page in a modern browser (e.g., Chrome, Edge), click "Start" to keep your screen on and switch to dark mode.
The "Stop" button will appear; click it to return to normal settings and light mode.
Ensure your browser supports the Screen Wake Lock API and use this feature while plugged in to avoid battery drain.
